What is Debt Mediation?

Debt Mediation is a structured negotiation process where we engage directly with your creditors on your behalf to find a fair and affordable solution to your outstanding debts. The goal is to reduce financial pressure, prevent legal action, and help you regain control of your finances.

Instead of struggling alone with overdue accounts, high interest rates, and collection calls, our team works to reach agreements that make repayment realistic and manageable for your current financial situation.

How Debt Mediation Helps You

  • Negotiates reduced settlement amounts with creditors
  • Prevents or stops legal action and garnishee orders
  • Consolidates multiple debts into affordable payment plans
  • Reduces interest rates and penalty fees where possible
  • Restores peace of mind and financial stability

Who Should Consider Debt Mediation?

Debt Mediation is ideal for individuals who are falling behind on payments, facing legal threats, or feeling overwhelmed by multiple debts but who are not yet under formal debt review.

  • People receiving legal letters or summons from creditors
  • Individuals with multiple overdue accounts
  • Anyone struggling to keep up with monthly repayments
  • Consumers seeking alternatives to debt review
